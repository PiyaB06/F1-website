About Aston Martin

Aston Martin is one of Britain's most iconic automotive manufacturers, renowned for combining luxury, elegance and high-performance engineering. Although the Aston Martin name first appeared in Formula One during the 1959 and 1960 seasons, the team returned to the championship in 2021 after Racing Point was rebranded under Aston Martin ownership. Since then, Aston Martin has invested heavily in becoming a genuine championship contender, constructing one of Formula One's most advanced factory campuses at Silverstone while attracting world-class engineers and drivers.

Tips for new fans: If you want to become an Aston Martin Fan in 2026, proceed with caution. The team is currently in a rebuilding phase™(a.k.a. failing miserably), and while they have the potential to become a championship contender, it may take time for them to achieve consistent success(points would be a good start).

The Aston Martin AMR26

Built for Formula One's revolutionary 2026 regulations, the Aston Martin AMR26 represents the beginning of a bold new era for the Silverstone-based team. Featuring Aston Martin's first works partnership with Honda, combined with Adrian Newey's legendary design philosophy, the AMR26 has been engineered around efficiency, aerodynamic excellence and sustainable hybrid power. Every component has been carefully designed to maximise performance under Formula One's newest technical regulations while maintaining Aston Martin's unmistakable elegance and precision.

The Aston Martin Vision

Aston Martin isn't simply trying to compete in Formula One— they're building for the future. Massive investment, world-class engineering talent and one of the newest facilities in the sport have transformed Aston Martin into one of Formula One's most ambitious projects.

🏭 World-Class Campus

Aston Martin's state-of-the-art Silverstone headquarters is one of Formula One's most advanced factories, featuring cutting-edge design, manufacturing and simulation facilities.

🧠 Adrian Newey

Widely regarded as Formula One's greatest designer, Adrian Newey joined Aston Martin to help shape the team's future under the 2026 regulations.

🇯🇵 Honda Partnership

Beginning in 2026, Aston Martin became Honda's official works team, giving them factory power units designed specifically for the new era.

💚 British Heritage

Aston Martin combines over a century of British automotive excellence with Formula One's most advanced technology.

🌿 Sustainable Innovation

The 2026 regulations place sustainability at the heart of Formula One, and Aston Martin has invested heavily in environmentally responsible engineering and manufacturing.

🏆 Championship Ambition

Every investment—from facilities to personnel—has one goal: transforming Aston Martin into a future Formula One World Champion.

Aston Martin Through The Years

1959

Aston Martin made its Formula One debut as a constructor.

The team scored its first World Championship points before leaving Formula One.

2021

Aston Martin entered Formula One's new regulation era with Honda power and the AMR26.
