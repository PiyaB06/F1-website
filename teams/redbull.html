About Red Bull Racing

Oracle Red Bull Racing may be one of Formula One's youngest teams, but it has quickly become one of the sport's greatest success stories. After purchasing the former Jaguar Racing team in 2005, Red Bull transformed from a bold newcomer into a championship-winning powerhouse.

Known for its fearless attitude, innovative engineering and aggressive racing philosophy, the team has consistently pushed the limits of what is possible in Formula One. Their partnership with legendary designer Adrian Newey produced some of the fastest and most dominant cars in the history of the sport.

If you're new to Formula One, Red Bull is often the team associated with spectacular overtakes, bold race strategies and relentless pursuit of victory. Whether they're fighting from the front or recovering from setbacks, they almost always make races exciting to watch.

The Red Bull RB22

Designed for Formula One's 2026 regulations, the Red Bull RB22 represents the next evolution of Adrian Newey's aerodynamic philosophy combined with Red Bull Powertrains' new partnership with Ford. Built around lighter chassis regulations, sustainable fuels and an all-new power unit, the RB22 aims to continue Red Bull Racing's legacy of building cars that are fast, efficient and aggressive. Every surface has been sculpted to maximize aerodynamic efficiency while maintaining the team's trademark high-speed cornering performance.

The Red Bull Formula

Since joining Formula One in 2005, Red Bull Racing has built its success around innovation, fearless racing and developing young talent. Here's what makes the team one of Formula One's most successful modern constructors.

🦬 Fearless Racing

Red Bull encourages aggressive overtaking and fighting for every position. The team's philosophy has always been to race boldly.

🏎️ Aerodynamic Excellence

Red Bull cars are famous for producing incredible downforce, allowing them to dominate high-speed corners.

👶 Junior Driver Programme

Drivers like Sebastian Vettel, Max Verstappen and many others developed through Red Bull's famous junior academy.

🧠 Aggressive Strategy

Whether it's undercuts, tyre strategy or daring pit calls, Red Bull rarely plays it safe on race day.

⚙️ Engineering Legacy

Adrian Newey's groundbreaking designs helped create some of Formula One's fastest and most dominant cars.

🌍 Global Brand

More than just a racing team, Red Bull has become one of the world's most recognizable sporting brands.

Red Bull Through The Years

2005

Red Bull Racing entered Formula One after purchasing Jaguar Racing.

2009

The RB5 became Red Bull's first genuine championship contender, winning six races.

2010

Sebastian Vettel secured Red Bull's first Drivers' and Constructors' Championships.

2011–2013

Vettel and Red Bull completed four consecutive championship doubles, establishing one of Formula One's greatest dynasties.

2021

Max Verstappen claimed his first World Championship in one of the most dramatic title battles in Formula One history.

2022–2024

Verstappen and Red Bull dominated the ground-effect era with record-breaking performances.

2026

Red Bull entered the new regulation era with the RB22 and its own Red Bull Ford Powertrains.
