About McLaren

McLaren is one of Formula One's most iconic and successful teams. Founded by New Zealander Bruce McLaren, the team has built a reputation for innovation, precision engineering and fearless racing. Over the decades, McLaren has been home to legendary drivers including Ayrton Senna, Alain Prost, Niki Lauda, Lewis Hamilton and many more. They are the reigning Driver's and Constructor's Champions. Today, the team has returned to the front of the grid with one of the strongest driver pairings in Formula One: Lando Norris and Oscar Piastri.

Tips for new fans: McLaren supporters have experienced everything from championship glory, to difficult rebuilding years, to an incredible return to the front. The papaya army is optimistic, loud and always believes the next race could be the one.
Speaking of, asking a McLaren fan what the Papaya rules are will either result in a long winded rant or a look of pure loathing aimed in your direction. Just google it dude.

The McLaren MCL40

Built for Formula One's 2026 regulations, the McLaren MCL40 continues the team's philosophy of aerodynamic efficiency, lightweight design and exceptional tyre management. Designed at the McLaren Technology Centre in Woking, the MCL40 represents decades of engineering excellence while embracing Formula One's newest hybrid era.

McLaren Through The Years

1963

Bruce McLaren founded Bruce McLaren Motor Racing in England, beginning one of Formula One's greatest success stories.

1968

McLaren claimed its first Formula One victory at the Belgian Grand Prix.

1974

Emerson Fittipaldi secured McLaren's first Drivers' Championship, while the team also won its first Constructors' title.

1988

Ayrton Senna and Alain Prost dominated Formula One with the legendary MP4/4, winning 15 of 16 races.

1998–1999

Mika Häkkinen delivered consecutive Drivers' Championships, marking another golden era for McLaren.

2008

Lewis Hamilton became Formula One World Champion in one of the most dramatic championship finishes in history.

2024–2026

McLaren returned to championship contention with Lando Norris and Oscar Piastri, entering Formula One's new regulation era stronger than ever.

The Triple Crown

McLaren is one of the very few names in motorsport associated with the legendary Triple Crown. The Triple Crown consists of:

  • 🏁 Monaco Grand Prix
  • 🏆 Indianapolis 500
  • 🇫🇷 24 Hours of Le Mans

Success across all three events represents the pinnacle of motorsport, showcasing excellence in Formula One, endurance racing and oval racing.

Did You Know?

Founded by a Driver

McLaren was founded by New Zealand racing driver Bruce McLaren, making it one of the few Formula One teams created by a driver.

Triple Crown Heritage

McLaren is one of the few teams with victories in Formula One, Indianapolis and Le Mans.

Papaya Returns

McLaren originally raced in papaya orange during the 1960s before bringing the iconic colour back in modern Formula One.
